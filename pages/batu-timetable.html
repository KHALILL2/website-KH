BATU Timetable

2024
Full-Stack Development

Overview

BATU Timetable is a comprehensive university scheduling platform designed specifically for Borg Al Arab Technological University. The system streamlines course management and automates timetable generation, making academic planning effortless for both students and administrators.

Key Features

  • Interactive course schedule builder
  • Automated conflict detection and resolution
  • Multi-year academic planning (Years 1-4)
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop
  • Real-time schedule updates
  • Export functionality for personal calendars

Technology Stack

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript (ES6+) Bootstrap Font Awesome

Impact

The platform has simplified the scheduling process for hundreds of students at BATU, reducing conflicts and making it easier to plan their academic journey. The intuitive interface ensures that even first-year students can quickly create their ideal timetable.

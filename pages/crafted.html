CRAFTED

2024
Branding & Design

Overview

CRAFTED is a comprehensive branding project for a modern furniture company that celebrates craftsmanship and contemporary design. The project encompasses brand identity, visual language, and user experience design to create a cohesive and memorable brand presence.

Project Scope

  • Brand identity development and logo design
  • Visual design system and style guide
  • User interface mockups for e-commerce platform
  • Product photography art direction
  • Marketing collateral and social media templates
  • Typography and color palette selection

Design Approach

Minimalist Design Modern Aesthetics User-Centric Brand Strategy Visual Identity

Key Achievements

The CRAFTED brand identity successfully communicates quality, sophistication, and modern living. The design system provides a flexible framework that scales across digital and physical touchpoints while maintaining brand consistency and visual appeal.

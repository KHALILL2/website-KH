GDG on Campus (BATU)

2024 - Present
Community Leadership

Overview

As the Founder and Organizer of Google Developer Group on Campus at Borg Al Arab Technological University, I've built a thriving tech community that brings together students passionate about technology, innovation, and collaborative learning.

Key Initiatives

  • Organized workshops on modern web development and cloud technologies
  • Hosted hackathons and coding competitions
  • Facilitated mentorship programs connecting students with industry professionals
  • Conducted study jams on Google technologies and certifications
  • Built partnerships with local tech companies for internship opportunities
  • Created inclusive learning environment for developers of all skill levels

Focus Areas

Web Development Cloud Computing Android Development Machine Learning Google Technologies Career Development

Impact

Through GDG on Campus, we've created a supportive ecosystem where students can learn, grow, and connect with like-minded individuals. Our initiatives have helped dozens of students land internships, build portfolios, and launch their tech careers.

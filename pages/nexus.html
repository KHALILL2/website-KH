What is NEXUS?

NEXUS is a specialized technical innovation team based at Borg Al Arab Technological University (BATU). It serves as a hub for advanced IoT solutions and campus infrastructure development, evolved from the 'Guardian Nodes' initiative into a comprehensive smart campus systems team.

Our Focus

We bridge the gap between hardware and software. Our primary focus is Smart Campus Solutions, specifically integrating physical security hardware (Turnstiles) with digital educational platforms (LMS). We design, prototype, and deploy systems that automate university operations.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To transform BATU into a fully automated, smart campus where technology enhances every aspect of the educational experience.

Mission: Creating seamless, secure ecosystems where physical access (gates) and digital presence (attendance/grades) are unified through intelligent automation.

Key Projects

  • Smart Turnstile V2: A mature, feature-rich physical access gate with enhanced security protocols. Integrates facial recognition, RFID authentication, and real-time access control systems.
  • LMS-Integrated Attendance System: A proprietary system that syncs physical gate entry directly with the university's Student Learning Management System for automated attendance tracking, eliminating manual processes.
